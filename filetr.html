FILLET CRAY IN PESTO AND PARMY HAM

With green beans and light mayonnaise. You can bake in an oven or fry in a pan. The fish fillet, anointed pesto (from the green of parsley and pistachios) wrapped in Parma ham is a quick way to prepare an elegant dinner. It does not have to be cod, maybe another white fish. To such a fish may well suit mayonnaise, for example, lemon. I made it by personally rubbing the yolk and adding a small amount of olive oil to it, as well as lemon zest.
